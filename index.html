Square Year
What Buy
A year as a unit, all at once. A series of days and some qualities, set in a proportionally equal array. Weeks and months are coordination tools. Their disproportions distort the experience of the year as a unit over time. 19 days ago was 19 days ago. The particular system of our time dates to 1582. We’ve had a day one and we’ve had a day ever since. Days resolve into positions without names. In the form of a square year.
